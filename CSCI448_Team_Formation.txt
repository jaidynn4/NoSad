App Name:
- NoSad

App Description:
- This app is intended to provide an easy and entertaining way for people 
struggling with depression to log and track their emotional state. This will be
accomplished by providing an interface to record and rate emotions throughout the day,
display customizable emotion trackers, deliver graphs and statistics of data to identify
low points, and offer support services by connecting with friends and family.
